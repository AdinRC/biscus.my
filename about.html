Vision

Create

create a world where cybersecurity is a fundamental aspect of daily life, where individuals are empowered to protect themselves and their data, and where organizations prioritize cybersecurity as a core business function.

Build

build a community where cybersecurity knowledge is freely shared, and individuals from diverse backgrounds and skill levels can come together to learn, collaborate, and innovate to improve cybersecurity for all.

Promote

promote a culture of cybersecurity consciousness, where individuals and organizations are motivated and supported to make cybersecurity a top priority in their personal and professional lives.

Mission

Educate

Developing educational resources and workshops to teach people about the importance of cybersecurity and how to protect themselves online

Hosting

Hosting community events to bring together individuals, businesses, and organizations to discuss cybersecurity challenges and collaborate on solutions

Partnership

Developing partnerships with cybersecurity experts, organizations, and government agencies to support and promote cybersecurity initiatives at a local and national level.
